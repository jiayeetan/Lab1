Mademoiselle Caroline Revière

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The potrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Revière was painted in 1806 by the French Ncoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Revière family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Musèe àu Louvre,Paris
